About Us

For over a century and a half, The The Christian Literature Society has been a beacon of enlightenment and knowledge. Established in 1858 by the dedicated Scottish Christian missionary, Dr. John Murdoch, our organization has been a symbol of spreading the message of faith and education through the powerful mediums of printing and publishing. Dr. Murdoch's visionary approach was evident in 1858 when he united the Christian Vernacular Education Society with the South India School Book Society and the Madras Religious Tract and Books Society. This momentous merger gave birth to 'The The Christian Literature Society' in 1891.
